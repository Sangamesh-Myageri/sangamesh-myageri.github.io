Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What services do you offer?

I offer full-stack web development, mobile app development, API design and development, cloud infrastructure setup, and technical consulting. I specialize in modern JavaScript frameworks and scalable backend architectures.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical web application takes 4-8 weeks, while larger enterprise solutions may require 3-6 months. I provide detailed estimates after understanding your requirements.

Do you work with teams or solo?

I'm comfortable working both independently and as part of a team. I have experience leading development teams and collaborating with designers, product managers, and other stakeholders.

What technologies do you prefer?

I primarily work with React, Node.js, Python, and modern cloud platforms like AWS. However, I'm technology-agnostic and choose the best tools for each project's specific requirements.

Are you available for remote work?

Yes, I'm available for both remote and on-site work. I have extensive experience working with distributed teams across different time zones and am comfortable with various collaboration tools.

How do you handle project communication?

I believe in clear and regular communication. I provide weekly progress updates, am available for scheduled calls or meetings, and use project management tools to keep everyone aligned on goals and timelines.
